City Palace (Jaipur) is a magnificent royal palace located in the heart of Jaipur. Built in the 18th century by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, it showcases a blend of Rajasthani and Mughal architecture. The palace complex includes courtyards, gardens, museums, and the famous Chandra Mahal and Mubarak Mahal, reflecting the rich heritage of Jaipur's royal family.
