Jaigarh Fort (Jaipur) is a historic hilltop fort located on the Aravalli Range, overlooking Amber Fort. Built in 1726 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, it was designed to protect Amber Palace. The fort is famous for housing the Jaivana Cannon, once the world's largest cannon on wheels, and showcases strong military architecture with thick walls and watchtowers.
