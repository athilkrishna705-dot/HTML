Jal Mahal (Jaipur) is a beautiful palace located in the middle of Man Sagar Lake. Built in the 18th century by Maharaja Madho Singh I, it showcases Rajput and Mughal architecture. The palace appears to float on water, with four floors submerged when the lake is full, making it one of Jaipur’s most scenic landmarks.
