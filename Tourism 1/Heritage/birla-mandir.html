Birla Mandir (Jaipur) is a beautiful Hindu temple made of white marble, located at the base of Moti Dungri Hill. Built in 1988 by the Birla family, the temple is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and Goddess Lakshmi. It is known for its modern architecture, peaceful atmosphere, and stunning carvings of Hindu gods along with figures from other religions.
