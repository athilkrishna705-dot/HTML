Sisodia Rani Garden (Jaipur) is a beautiful royal garden located on the Jaipur-Agra road. Built in 1728 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II for his queen Sisodia Rani, the garden features terraced lawns, fountains, pavilions, and wall paintings depicting scenes from Radha-Krishna. It reflects Mughal and Rajput garden architecture and offers a peaceful retreat from the city.
