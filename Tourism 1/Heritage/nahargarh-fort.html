Nahargarh Fort (Jaipur) is a historic fort located on the Aravalli Hills, overlooking the city of Jaipur. Built in 1734 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, it was originally named Sudarshangarh. The fort served as a defense for the city and is famous today for its scenic views, architecture, and sunset points.
